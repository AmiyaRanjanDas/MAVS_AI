Exposing the Hidden Risks in GenAI

From data leakage to prompt attacks, Mavs AI uncovers and mitigates GenAI risks in real-time.

Sensitive Data Leakage

Prompts sent to LLMs can unknowingly carry PII, financials, and sensitive business data. Without runtime defense, this data silently leaks, escalating security and compliance risk.

Prompt Injection & Jailbreaks

Attackers can exploit GenAI by crafting prompts that bypass security, access sensitive data, or trigger unauthorized actions. Without runtime guardrails, even trusted apps become vulnerable.

No Visibility into AI Usage

Without visibility into GenAI interactions, enterprises lose track of how AI is being used. Data leaks, policy violations, and misuse go undetected, creating silent risk across AI workflows.

Policy Non-compliance

Unmonitored GenAI use can bypass data handling policies and expose sensitive information, leading to legal liability, regulatory penalties, and reputational damage.

Every GenAI Prompt is a Potential Breach

Indirect Prompt Injection

Malicious data hidden in links, PDFs, or websites manipulates the AI when users reference external content

Data Poisoning

Training data manipulation can cause AI models to produce biased or malicious outputs that compromise security

Model Extraction

Attackers query the model repeatedly to steal proprietary AI logic and replicate it without authorization

Sensitive Data Exposure

User prompts containing PII or confidential information can leak through logs, training data, or model outputs

Jailbreak Attacks

Carefully crafted prompts bypass safety controls to make the AI generate prohibited or harmful content

Lack of Visibility

No insight into AI usage - What data is being shared, where it is going, or in what context
